Use References

Some people may think that using references is cheating, when really that's not the case. It's how you learn to learn to draw things like body anatomy and nature. You're not born knowing how to draw everything after all.


Drawing hands practice

For example, you may want to learn how to draw hands. You can google pictures of real life hands or drawings of hands. Or you can use your own hands as a reference. This is how people do studies of things that they want to practice, and it is not really cheating or plagarism.
