Use References

Some people may think that using references is cheating, when really that's not the case. It's how you learn to learn to draw things like body anatomy and nature. You're not born knowing how to draw everything after all.

18 APR, 2020

Copy Others

Much like referencing, some people may think that copying others is also cheating. It's only cheating if you copy someone and then claim it as your own. If you credit the artist, you're good.

18 APR, 2020

Limit Your Color Palette

Limiting your palette makes the piece feel more cohesive as a whole. If you try to use every color, it won't look as nice and it will be distracting.

18 APR, 2020

Have Fun

Just have fun with your artwork and experiment. You'll become a great artist eventually!
